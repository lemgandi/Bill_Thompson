Campbells, Luggs & Blackwells of Nelson, PA

1948 Sitka, Alaska Postcard From Treva Hooker Hazlett to Jessica Hoyt Thompson

I'm many miles from Sitka, nearer Russia. Just a hamlet, 200 or so, on the bank of the Yukon. It's in gold country. I'd like a nugget.

Treva
